How to Turn Food Scraps into Manure

Transforming your food scraps into rich compost is a great way to reduce waste and nourish your garden...

  • Collect vegetable peels, eggshells, coffee grounds (avoid meat/dairy).
  • Use a compost bin with proper airflow.
  • Mix green (wet) and brown (dry) materials for balance.
  • Turn the pile every week for aeration.
  • In a few months, you'll have dark, earthy compost!
